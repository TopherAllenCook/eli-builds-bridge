Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Eli Builds stores very little: at most, the ID of a comment you left on our posts (used only to avoid messaging you twice) and any Direct Messages you chose to send us. You can have all of it deleted at any time.

How to request deletion

  1. Email eli.builds.ai@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request."
  2. Include the Instagram username you used to interact with us so we can locate any records.
  3. We will delete all information associated with your account and confirm by email within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Stored comment IDs linked to your interactions
  • Any Direct Message history on our side

Note: content you posted on Instagram or Facebook itself is controlled by you through those platforms. To remove a comment or message from your own account, use Instagram's or Facebook's built-in controls.
