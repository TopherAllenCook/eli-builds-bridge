Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Eli Builds ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Eli Builds Instagram and Facebook presence and an automated assistant that responds to people who interact with our posts. This policy explains what information we handle and why. By commenting on our posts or messaging us, you agree to this policy.

1. Information we handle

When you publicly comment on an Eli Builds Instagram or Facebook post, we may receive, through Meta's official Platform APIs:

  • Your public comment text and the comment's ID
  • Your Instagram username and Instagram-scoped user ID
  • Messages you choose to send us in Direct Messages

We do not collect your email, phone number, location, contacts, or any data beyond what Meta's APIs provide for the interaction you initiated.

2. How we use it

  • To reply to a request you made. If you comment a keyword (for example, "GO") asking us to send you a link or resource, we use it to send you a single Direct Message with that link.
  • To avoid messaging you twice. We store the comment ID so our automation does not send you the same reply more than once.
  • To respond to conversations you start in our Direct Messages.

3. What we do not do

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your information.
  • We do not use your information for advertising profiles.
  • We do not message people who have not interacted with our content.

4. Sharing

We use Meta Platforms (Instagram and Facebook) to deliver messages, and standard infrastructure providers to operate our automation. We share information only as needed to provide the service you requested or as required by law. Your interactions are also subject to Instagram's and Meta's own privacy policies.

5. Data retention & deletion

We retain comment IDs only as long as needed to prevent duplicate messages, and we delete them on a rolling basis. You can request deletion of any information we hold about you at any time, see our Data Deletion Instructions.

6. Children

Our content and services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

8. Contact

Questions or requests: eli.builds.ai@gmail.com
